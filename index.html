Our Pool Work

Prepare, Construct, and Polish

  1. Phase 1: Prepare your pool.

    In the first phase of your pool remodel, we ensure that we open a dialogue with you about your vision for the pool and get the tiles and other finishes. We begin the pool draining process.

  2. Phase 2: Construct your pool.

    In the second phase of your pool remodel, we start hydroblasting, a technique to remove the old damaged plaster material from the gunite without damaging it. Next, we install the main drainer and skimmer equalizer lines. Ensuring that the pools' treatment equipment can efficiently treat your pool's water.

  3. Phase 3: Polish and Finish your pool.

    In the final phase of your pool remodel, we start the artisan work of carefully tiling your pool. We offer the opportunity to truly make your new pool unique with a custom tile design. We also lay any other featurettes to your pool, such as brick-housing for your hot tub. Our final step is to apply the plaster to ensure your pool looks glorious for years to come!
